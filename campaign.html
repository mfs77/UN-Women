This ad campaign brings a new light to traditional gender equality campaigns. Using flat graphics instead of the traditional still life photography, the simple graphics draw the eye in and make it simple to understand what the campaign is discussing. This campaign was created to inform viewers of the inequalities that women experience in their everyday lives. This was achieved using a simple color palette of the traditional blue and pink to represent the genders and simple flat graphics.
